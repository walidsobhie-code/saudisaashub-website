## ✅ Article Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Title is compelling (Arabic)
- [ ] Excerpt ≤ 150 characters
- [ ] Keywords included (5-10)
- [ ] H1 → H2 → H3 hierarchy
- [ ] At least 3 sections (H2)
- [ ] Bullet/numbered lists for readability
- [ ] Internal links to related articles
- [ ] CTA at the end
- [ ] Tags at bottom
- [ ] Author bio
- [ ] Publication date
- [ ] Reading time
```
