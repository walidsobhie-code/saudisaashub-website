# SEO & Content Strategy - SaudiSaaSHub

## 🎯 Article Template

Each article should follow this structure:

### 1. Front Matter (JSON-LD for SEO)
```json
{
  "title": "Main Title (Arabic) | English",
  "slug": "url-friendly-slug",
  "excerpt": "150 characters max",
  "keywords": "keyword1, keyword2, keyword3",
  "category": "Category Name",
  "date": "YYYY-MM-DD",
  "author": "Author Name",
  "readTime": X
}
```

### 2. Article Body Structure

```
# H1: Main Title (Arabic + English)

**بقلم:** [Author] | [Date]

---

## Hook (1 sentence)
[Compelling opening that captures attention]

---

## مقدمة: [Topic]

[2-3 sentences explaining article scope]

---

## H2: Section 1

### H3: Subsection (if needed)

[Paragraphs...]

- Bullet point 1
- Bullet point 2
- Bullet point 3

---

## H2: Section 2

[More content...]

---

## H2: Section 3

[Final section...]

---

## 💡 الخلاصة

[Wrap up + CTA]

---

### 📊 Related

[Link to related articles]
```

---

## 📋 Content Checklist

| Item | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Title (H1) | ✅ | Arabic + clear |
| Excerpt | ✅ | ≤150 chars |
| Keywords | ✅ | 5-10, Arabic + English |
| Sections (H2) | ✅ | Min 3 sections |
| Bullet Lists | ✅ | For readability |
| Tables | ✅ | For comparisons |
| Internal Links | ✅ | To related articles |
| CTA | ✅ | End of article |
| Tags | ✅ | Hashtags |
| Author | ✅ | Name + date |
| Read Time | ✅ | Estimate |
